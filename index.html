My name is Phung(foom), I recently graduated with a Bachelors in Data Science from the University of Houston - Downtown. I enjoy learning workflows and figuring out where to focus for optimization. My projects and undergrad research highlight the aspects of data science I want to keep building on. In my undergrad research role,I worked in R preprocessing Likert survey data from an IRB-approved Criminal Justice study, then produced the group summaries and quantitative results the faculty team used in their writeups. My senior project, a CNN classifier for potato leaf diseases, went beyond classification by using Explainable AI (XAI) to surface what the model was actually attending to in each image. The point of that work is giving domain experts (plant pathologists, in this case) something they can interpret and push back on, which makes the communication side just as critical as the technical side. I also dove into past research to see how model architectures vary and where current work is focused, staying current with a fast evolving field. I work well in teams, value clear communication, and enjoy environments where information moves freely between people. Open to data analyst and data scientist opportunities, feel free to reach out.
